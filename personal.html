Hobbies

A selection of the definitely not work-related hobbies I enjoy the most :)

Textile crafts

I am a passionate crafter and I have a particular inclination for everything textile-related. I especially enjoy crocheting, knitting and sewing, which I think reach their full joyful potential when shared. In this picture, the awesome ESO crochet club on Secret Santa day in 2022.

Skating

I really enjoy inline skating, even though I'm by no means an expert - I just learned how to brake on my own a couple of months ago! In this picture I'm at one of the awesome Munich Blade Nights with some ESO and Max Planck friends in July 2023.

Hiking

I spent countless summers in my childhood and as a teenager in the beautiful italian Alps, so I'm very happy whenever I get the chance to go hiking near the austrian border or around one of the many lakes in Bavaria. This picture is from a winter hike in January 2023, with the whole ESO gang.
