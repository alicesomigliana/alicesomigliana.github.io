Welcome!

Welcome to my homepage! My name is Alice Somigliana and I am a second-year PhD student at the European Southern Observatory (ESO) in Garching bei Munchen, Germany. Thank you for stopping by, I hope you enjoy your time here!

About me

I was born and raised in beautiful Italy, where I studied for both my Bachelor's and Master's degrees. To pursue my passion for astrophysics and embark on the amazing adventure that is the PhD, I moved to Germany in September 2021. You can find more about my academic path in the My research section.

In my free time, I enjoy a lot of crafty activities such as crocheting, knitting and sewing. I like to practice many sports, without being a pro in any of them, just for fun. This includes hiking, volleyball, skating and biking. For a description of my hobbies and interests, see Personal.
